Welcome

Lusaka's Hidden Gem
on Lilayi Road

Anina's Executive Lodge is a peaceful, well-appointed retreat nestled in the quiet suburban area of Chilanga, just 12km from Lusaka's city centre. Set within lush tropical gardens, we offer the perfect balance of comfort, convenience, and warm Zambian hospitality.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, our lodge provides spacious rooms, a beautiful outdoor pool, an on-site restaurant, and everything you need for a truly relaxing stay.

We are located on Plot 29B, Lilayi Road — easily accessible and close to Lusaka Kafue Road, making us an ideal base for both local and international visitors.
