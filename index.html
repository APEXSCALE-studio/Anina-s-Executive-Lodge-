Welcome

Lusaka's Hidden Gem
on Lilayi Road

Anina's Executive Lodge is a peaceful retreat nestled in Chilanga, just 12km from Lusaka's city centre. Set within lush tropical gardens, we offer the perfect balance of comfort, convenience, and warm Zambian hospitality.

Whether here for business or leisure, our lodge provides spacious rooms, a beautiful outdoor pool, an on-site restaurant, and everything you need for a relaxing stay.

Located on Plot 29B, Lilayi Road — easily accessible from Kafue Road, ideal for local and international visitors.
